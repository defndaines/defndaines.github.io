Osso Bucco

Pressure Cooker. From Dad Cooks Dinner.

Ingredients

  • 3 lb beef shank
  • 3 Tbsp kosher salt
  • 1½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 stalk celery, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • ½ tsp kosher salt
  • 1 cup chicken stock (reduce if less meat)
  • ½ cup dry white wine
  • 1 can diced tomatoes

Method

Cook on high for 35 minutes. Natural release for 15 minutes.
