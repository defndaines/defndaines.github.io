肉じゃが (Nikujaga)

Pressure Cooker. From Just One Cookbook.

Ingredients

  • 1 carrot
  • 2 potatoes, cut into eighths (soak 10 min in cold water)
  • 1 package shirataki noodles (7 oz)
  • ½ lb thinly sliced beef (chuck or rib eye)
  • 1 Tbsp neutral oil (vegetable, canola, etc.)
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup dashi
  • 3 Tbsp mirin
  • 2 Tbsp sake
  • 3 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 10 green beans (blanch separately, add at the end)

Method

Cook on Meat/Stew setting for 15 minutes, quick release. Add blanched green beans at the end.
