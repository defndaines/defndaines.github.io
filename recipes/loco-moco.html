Loco Moco

From Girls Can Grill.

Ingredients

Hamburger

  • 1 lb ground beef, at least 15% fat content, seasoned with salt and pepper

Gravy

  • 1 Tbsp flour
  • 1½ cups beef broth
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp butter

Topping

  • 4 eggs, fried sunny-side up

Method

Cook hamburgers first, then use the rendered fat to make the gravy.
