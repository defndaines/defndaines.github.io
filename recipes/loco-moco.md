# Loco Moco

From https://girlscangrill.com/recipe/loco-moco/

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef, at least 15% fat content, season with salt and pepper

- 1 Tbsp flour
- 1.5 cup beef broth
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp butter

- 4 eggs, fried sunny-side up

## Method

Cook hamburgers first, then use the fat to cook the gravy.
