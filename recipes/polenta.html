Rice Cooker Polenta

From Allrecipes.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp butter (at least)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 cup broth or water
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup polenta
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 4 oz shredded cheese (mix Parmesan and cheddar, or improvise)
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 8–10 mushrooms, chopped

Method

Throw it all in the rice cooker and cook.
