Butter Chicken

Instant Pot. Adapted from Two Sleevers.

Ingredients

In the Instant Pot

  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 2 tsp minced ginger
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Cayenne or diced pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 lb boneless chicken thighs

Add all ingredients to pot. Cook 10 minutes on high, natural release.

After Pressure Release

  • 1 stick butter
  • 4 oz heavy cream or coconut milk

After release, add the above and allow to melt and mix.

Garnish

  • Chopped cilantro

Garam Masala

  • 6 parts coriander seeds
  • 2 parts cumin seeds
  • 1 part cloves
  • 1 part cardamom seeds
  • Cinnamon
  • Red chile peppers
  • Bay leaves
