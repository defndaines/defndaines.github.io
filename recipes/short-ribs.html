Short Ribs 牛バラ肉と大根煮

Pressure Cooker. From Just One Cookbook.

Ingredients

  • 1 knob ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3-inch piece daikon radish
  • 1 carrot
  • 1¾ lb boneless short ribs (800 g)
  • Kosher or sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp roasted sesame oil
  • 2 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 Tbsp sake
  • 4 Tbsp mirin
  • 4 Tbsp soy sauce

Method

Cube the meat and sear on all sides. Cook on Meat/Stew setting for 35 minutes. Quick release.
